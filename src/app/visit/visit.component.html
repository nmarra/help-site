What is HELP?

HELP is a non-profit Therapeutic Riding Center for people with disabilities. We teach horsemanship and horseback riding skills to improve life skills.

HELP has been serving the Austin area since 1984 on our beautiful 20 acre facility just outside of Pflugerville. We not only use our arena, but are blessed with a 5 acre natural riding trail and a barn built by the Corps of Engineers in 1999. Contact us today to schedule a tour.

Hours of Operation

HELP is open Tuesday - Saturday each week from 9am-6pm, seasonal hours may apply. To protect the privacy of our riders please contact us to schedule a visit/tour. Please contact us for more information.
