How to Help

Your monthly or one-time donations are critical to HELP's success. Use the Donate button for a one-time donation, or choose a donation option and then click the Subscribe button to donate monthly.

Our program is vital to many people with disabilities in our community. We invite you to visit our facility to see the impact your gift is having on their lives. As a federally registered 501(c)(3) non-profit, all donations are tax deductible.

You can help by donating to Horse Empowered Learning Programs. Your donations help us to care for our therapy horses. Choose the Donate button for a one time donation. Choose the Subscribe button to make recurring monthly donations.

There are many options available to help this organization. See below.

Donation Options

HELP with Amazon

Whenever you order anything from Amazon, remember to type in "smile." before "amazon" to support HELP. Click here to set it up! Select the one that says "Manor, TX".
We have a wish list on amazon.com as well, because we are always in need of tack, fencing materials, bedding, and more.
If you have some horse or farm equipment that you'd like to donate, please email us at info@helpcenterofaustin.org.

Horse Sponsorship

Horse Sponsorship includes covering the costs for veterinary, feed, hay, hoof, and supplements for each of our special horses. You can select Rooster, Mistee, Jake, or the donkeys (a two-for-one deal) to support! This is our largest cost and your support of our special horses impacts all of their rider's lives for years to come. We will recognize you on our website, social media, and more throughout the year, including pictures and updates.

Use the subscribe button to sponsor your horse. Note the name of your sponsored horse in the notes/comments.

Tractor and Shredder Fund

Use our GoFundMe account to donate directly to our tractor/shredder fund. We are desperately in need of equipment to keep our pastures in a safe condition for our horses and riders.

Donate your Old Car

Donate your old car, boat, or truck to HELP. It's easy! You can donate online or call 877-999-8322 to make your donation.

Don't donate your car or truck to some charity you have never heard of. This trusted service makes sure your vehicle is properly handled so you get your tax deduction and HELP gets the benefit of your donation.

General Fund

Helps us cover a variety of our daily and monthly expenses including facility maintenance, volunteer supplies, facility rent, lesson supplies, and more!

Volunteering

Service project days for your group and ongoing opportunities available in and out of the arena. Facilities, horse care, office work, and more!

Employer Matching

Check with your HR department to see if they match your donations to HELP! Click these links to see if your employer is one of the top who donates for your volunteer time and/or donation matches.

Spread the Word

Share this website and are social media posts with friends or family members who might be willing to help. We're on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and more!
